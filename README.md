## 📌 **PROJECT OVERVIEW**

This project presents a **professional Sales Analytics Dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**, covering **2 years of sales data (2023–2024)** for a multi-service digital agency operating across the UAE. The dashboard consolidates over **$3 million in total sales** into a single, interactive executive view — enabling sales managers, marketing teams, and business owners to track revenue trends, monitor margin performance, analyze customer acquisition channels, and benchmark top-performing clients in real time.

Built using **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and advanced Excel formulas**, the dashboard demonstrates that powerful BI solutions don't always require specialized tools — Excel, when mastered, is a complete analytics platform.

## ⚙️ **METHODOLOGY & WORKFLOW**

```text
📥 Raw Data Import  ──►  🧹 Data Cleaning  ──►  📐 Pivot Table Setup
                                                        │
                                                        ▼
📤 Dashboard Finalization  ◄──  🎨 Chart Design  ◄──  🔢 KPI Formula Cards
```

### 🔷 **Step 1 — Data Collection & Import**
Imported raw transactional sales data into Excel covering 2 full years. Structured the data as a formatted **Excel Table** (`Ctrl+T`) to enable dynamic Pivot Table refresh and slicer connectivity.

### 🔷 **Step 2 — Data Cleaning & Standardization**
- Standardized date formats to `DD/MM/YYYY` for consistent month/year extraction
- Validated customer names, city labels, and service type categories for grouping accuracy
- Removed duplicate entries and corrected formatting inconsistencies across columns

### 🔷 **Step 3 — KPI Card Formulas**
Built 5 dynamic KPI cards using Excel formulas referencing the data table:
- **Total Sales** → `SUMIFS()` filtered by year slicer selection
- **Total Margin** → `SUMIFS()` on margin column
- **Average Margin %** → `AVERAGEIF()` across all transactions
- **New Customers** → `COUNTIF()` on customer type = "New"
- **Repeat Customers** → `COUNTIF()` on customer type = "Repeat"

### 🔷 **Step 4 — Pivot Tables & Charts**
Created 7 separate Pivot Tables, each powering a dedicated chart:
- Monthly sales trend line chart (2023 & 2024 overlaid)
- Customer source grouped bar chart (Ads / Organic / Walk-in by year)
- Sales by city pie chart
- Sales by service type bar chart
- Department margin donut chart
- New vs Repeat sales dual-line chart
- Top 10 Customers horizontal bar table

### 🔷 **Step 5 — Dashboard Assembly & Design**
Assembled all charts and KPI cards on a dedicated **Dashboard sheet** with a navy-and-white professional theme. Linked all visuals to a **Year Slicer** (2023 / 2024) so every element updates simultaneously on selection.

## 🔍 **KEY INSIGHTS & FINDINGS**

> 💡 *Data-backed sales insights extracted from the dashboard — suitable for sales reviews, quarterly business reports, and strategy discussions.*

- 💰 **Total revenue of $3,054,204 was generated across 2023–2024** with a healthy average margin of **30.63%** — indicating strong profitability relative to cost of delivery.

- 📈 **2024 shows a significant sales spike from May onward**, crossing the $200K/month mark for the first time — driven by an identifiable surge that warrants investigation into which channels or campaigns triggered this growth.

- 🏙️ **Dubai dominates city-level sales at 30%**, followed by Abu Dhabi at 26% — together accounting for **over half of all revenue**. Sharjah at 13% represents a market with the most room to grow.

- 🔍 **SEO is the highest-grossing service line at ~$800K**, outperforming App Dev, Web Dev, Social Media, and Ads — making it the core revenue engine and the most critical service to retain and scale.

- 🏛️ **Marketing department contributes 47% of total margin** — nearly half of all profit — while Design contributes only 22%, signaling a potential opportunity to review Design's pricing or cost structure.

- 🔁 **Repeat customers (298) outnumber New customers (182) by 64%**, and repeat sales consistently outperform new sales month-over-month — confirming strong customer loyalty and the effectiveness of retention over pure acquisition.

- 🌐 **Organic channel is the top revenue driver** across both years, followed closely by Walk-in — suggesting the agency's reputation and word-of-mouth are stronger acquisition drivers than paid Ads spend.

- 🏆 **Vantoro Enterprises is the #1 client at $190,001**, with the Top 10 customers collectively representing a significant share of total revenue — highlighting key account concentration risk and the need for account diversification.

## 🚀 **HOW TO USE THIS DASHBOARD**

> 📥 **Step 1** — Download and open `Sales_Analytics_Dashboard.xlsx` in **Microsoft Excel** (2016 or later recommended)

> 🎛️ **Step 2** — Use the **Year Slicer** (top-left) to toggle between **2023**, **2024**, or both years simultaneously

> 📊 **Step 3** — All KPI cards, charts, and the Top 10 table will update **automatically** based on the slicer selection

> 🖱️ **Step 4** — Hover over chart data points for **tooltip values**, and click pie/donut segments to highlight individual slices

> ✏️ **Step 5** — To add new data, simply append rows to the **raw data sheet** and refresh all Pivot Tables (`Data → Refresh All`)

### 🔴 **S — SITUATION**

> A UAE-based digital agency with operations across 5 cities and 5 service lines had **no centralized view of sales performance**. Sales data for 2023–2024 was scattered across multiple sheets with no aggregation, no trend visibility, and no way to quickly answer questions like *"Which city generates the most revenue?"*, *"Are repeat customers growing?"*, or *"Which department is the most profitable?"* — decisions were being made without data.

---

### 🟠 **T — TASK**

> As the Data Analyst, I was responsible for:
> - **Designing and building a fully interactive Excel dashboard** from raw transactional data
> - **Defining and calculating 5 executive KPIs** — Total Sales, Total Margin, Avg Margin %, New vs Repeat Customers
> - **Creating 8 visualizations** covering trends, geography, service lines, departments, and customer rankings
> - **Enabling dynamic filtering** via Year Slicer so stakeholders could self-serve without analyst support
> - **Delivering actionable insights** that could directly inform sales strategy, marketing budget, and account management

---

### 🟡 **A — ACTION**

> **1. 🧹 Data Structuring** — Converted raw data into a formatted Excel Table for dynamic Pivot Table connectivity and reliable slicer behavior across all dashboard elements.

> **2. 🔢 KPI Engineering** — Built 5 dynamic KPI cards using `SUMIFS()`, `COUNTIF()`, and `AVERAGEIF()` formulas — all linked to the slicer so they update automatically on year selection.

> **3. 📐 Pivot Table Architecture** — Designed 7 individual Pivot Tables, each structured for a specific business question — monthly trend, city split, service type, department margin, customer source, new vs repeat, and top 10 rankings.
